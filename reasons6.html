Part 6 - Money is Evil Reasons

  • Money creates artificial scarcity to increase profits despite abundant resources
  • It enables exploitation of workers through wage suppression and poor working conditions
  • Financial systems perpetuate debt cycles that trap individuals and nations
  • Money allows wealthy individuals to influence political decisions through lobbying
  • It drives environmental destruction as corporations prioritize profits over sustainability
  • Financial speculation creates economic instability that affects ordinary people
  • Money enables tax evasion by the wealthy, reducing public services for everyone else
  • It creates arms races in education and housing, making basic needs unaffordable
  • Wealth concentration leads to social unrest and political instability
  • Money promotes short-term thinking over long-term sustainability and planning
