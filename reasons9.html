Part 9 - Fashion and Spending Reasons

  • Fashion industry practices contribute to water pollution through textile dyeing
  • Fast fashion exploits workers in developing countries with poor working conditions
  • Consumer spending on trends creates financial stress and debt for families
  • Fashion marketing manipulates self-esteem to drive unnecessary purchases
  • The industry generates massive textile waste as clothes are quickly discarded
  • Brand obsession replaces genuine self-expression with corporate messaging
  • Seasonal fashion cycles pressure people to constantly update their wardrobes
  • Fashion trends promote unsustainable resource consumption and environmental damage
  • Impulse buying undermines long-term financial planning and security
  • Fast fashion contributes to microplastic pollution in oceans and ecosystems
